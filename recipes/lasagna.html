A Simple Lasagna Recipe

Why did the tomato turn red? Because it saw the lasagna without the sauce!

Lasagna, It's not just any pasta. It's layers upon layers of saucy, cheesy goodness that'll make your crap your pants.

If you're looking for a carb-filled, belly warming smack in the face! Lasagna is your go-to.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ground Beef or Italian Sausage
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps: Mamma, mia

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna pasta according to package instructions until they're al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add chopped onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  4. Add the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and eggs.
  6. Spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Then add a layer of lasagna pasta, followed by a layer of the cheese mixture, and a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
